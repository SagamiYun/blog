## Kit Kyo

**Full-stack engineer · DevOps · agent development.**

I build and ship across the whole stack — from product front-end and back-end, through the DevOps that keeps it all running, to LLM-driven **agents** and the tooling that makes them reliable. Lately I'm putting real time into the layers underneath: **machine learning** and **infrastructure**.

### What I do

- **Full-stack engineering** — front-end, back-end, APIs, databases — owning a feature end to end.
- **DevOps** — CI/CD, containers, cloud, networking, observability; keeping systems healthy and shippable.
- **Agent development** — building LLM-driven agents: tool use, orchestration, memory, and the plumbing around them.

### What I'm learning

- **Machine learning** — moving from *using* models to understanding and training them (MoE, world models, RL).
- **Infrastructure** — distributed systems, GPUs/compute, and the substrate ML actually runs on.
